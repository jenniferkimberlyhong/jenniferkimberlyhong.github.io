Hi! I'm Jen.

I'm a Korean-American UX designer based in New York city area.

I work daily with clients to understand their business requirements to provide agile solutions on software implementations. Working on these projects, motivates me to continue working with clients to understand their problems and bring innovative design solutions that will make user tasks easier and working with technology fun.

To further my education, I pursued an apprenticeship at Bloc to build my skills and knowledge in UX/UI design and frontend development. At the multinational technology firm, I work on large team projects where we utilize cloud storage application to share our work and collaborate. At Bloc, I worked on user research, designing, testing, and prototyping a responsive web and mobile application for cloud storage product utilizing my daily personal experience in addition to the challenges faced by users. My experiences in package solution consulting combined with my studies in user experience design will help bring a different creative perspective in solving problems.
