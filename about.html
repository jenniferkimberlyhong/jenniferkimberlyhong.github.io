Hi, I'm Jen!

I'm a Korean-American UX Designer and Technology Consultant based in New York City. I'm currently a Senior Consultant in Product and Technology at a Multinational Technology firm. For the past 6 years, I have been working on client-facing projects in various roles from Product Lead Analyst, Test Lead, to Learning Experience Design Lead in the Investment Banking, Insurance, Retail, and Consumer industry. I work daily with clients to understand their business requirements and challenges to develop innovative solutions to provide an engaging and seamless user experience. I enjoy collaborating and working to improve the lives of others through creative designs.
